How to Play

Head Trip is a cooperative perspective-taking game. One player creates a room and shares the code. Everyone joins on their own device.

Each question: The Tripper sees a Head Card (a role) and picks the answer that person would choose. Everyone else debates, then votes on what they think the Tripper picked.

Chips: Everyone starts with 3. Voting costs 1 chip. Get it right = keep your chip. Wrong = lose it. If everyone's right, the Tripper earns +1. If everyone's wrong, the Tripper loses 1.

Out of chips? You still debate but can't vote until a Tripper bonus round.
